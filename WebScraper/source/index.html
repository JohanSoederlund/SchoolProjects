Rules

The rules are simple. First you need to fill in your username below. When you press enter a question will appear and you will have 20 seconds to answer it correctly. If you get the answer right a new question will be presented until you either win by getting all the questions right or lose by either an incorrect answer or exceeding the time-limit. Your final result will then be presented to you and you may try again.
